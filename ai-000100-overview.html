4  4. AI in Everyday Use

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has made significant advancements in recent years, with Large Language Models (LLMs) (e.g. chatgpt) and generative AI leading the way in transforming how we interact with data and technology. Below, we explore some key areas where AI is making an impact. Many or most of these tools can be used for free (sometimes with limited features). Most tools also have a “paid” plan which allows for additional features, for quicker responses, etc.

AI continues to rapidly evolve, with advancements in personalization, automation, and ethical considerations. As AI integrates further into everyday life, it will enhance how we work with data, make decisions, and create content.

Note that the content on this page itself was generated by chatgpt.com (in Feb 2025) and edited by me. I did not confirm that all of the links work but if they don’t I recommend searching online for the current links.

4.1 General purpose LLM Chat Websites

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI-powered tools capable of understanding and generating human-like text. Here are some well-known platforms where users can interact with these models. These are all roughly similar. Their features keep evolving. For example as of this writing ChatGPT could generate picture while Claude.ai could not. Exactly what is possible with each model might also depend on whether you are using a free version or are paying a fee.

  • ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)

    This is a good overview of what is possible with chatgpt.com as of Feb 2025. I recommend that you watch (perhaps on double speed) even if you are already familiar with chatGPT.com.

    https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PDw3Uk9dN9k&t=521s

  • Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)

  • Claude (claude.ai) - Developed by Anthropic

  • Grok (grok.com) - Developed by X (formerly twitter)

4.3 Image Generation (i.e. pictures)

  • DALL·E (openai.com/dall-e) - AI-powered image generation from text descriptions.

  • Midjourney (midjourney.com) - Specializes in high-quality AI-generated art.

  • Stable Diffusion (stablediffusionweb.com) - Open-source image generation AI.

  • chatgpt.com also has the ability to generate pictures (see link above)

4.4 Music and Audio Generation

  • Suno AI (suno.ai) - AI-generated music based on text prompts.

  • Boomy (boomy.com) - Create and personalize AI-generated music.

4.5 Video Generation

  • Runway (runwayml.com) - AI-powered video editing and generation.

  • Synthesia (synthesia.io) - AI-generated videos with avatars and voice synthesis.

4.6 Code Generation and Assistance

  • GitHub Copilot (github.com/features/copilot) - AI-powered coding assistance.

  • Replit Ghostwriter (replit.com) - AI-powered coding suggestions for developers.

4.7 AI for Writing and Content Creation

  • Jasper (jasper.ai) - AI-generated marketing copy, blog posts, and content.

  • Copy.ai (copy.ai) - Helps generate creative and engaging text for various use cases.
